🔹 What is a PLC?

A Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) is an industrial digital computer designed for the control of manufacturing processes, machinery, and automation systems. PLCs are ruggedized, capable of operating in harsh environments, and are programmed using IEC 61131-3 languages. They continuously monitor inputs, execute logic, and update outputs in a cyclic process called the scan cycle.

🔹 Scan Time Calculator

Scan time is the time required for a PLC to read inputs, execute the user program, and update outputs. It affects system response time and performance. Typical scan times range from 1 ms to 100 ms depending on the PLC and program size.
