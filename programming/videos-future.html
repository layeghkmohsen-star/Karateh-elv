🔹 Future Trends in Industrial Programming

  • IEC 61499 and model-based, portable control logic
  • OPC UA as the unifying protocol for IT/OT convergence
  • Low-code and simulation-driven PLC development environments
  • Containerized and virtualized PLC/SCADA runtimes
  • Cybersecurity-hardened programming practices for control systems

🔹 What's Next in Industrial Programming?

The future of industrial programming will be shaped by open standards, virtualization, and tighter integration between control systems and enterprise IT. Programmers will increasingly need skills in networking, cybersecurity, and data engineering alongside traditional PLC, HMI, and SCADA development to support smart manufacturing and Industry 4.0 initiatives.
