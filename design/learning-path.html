🗺️ Design Learning Path

A structured learning path to become a skilled engineering designer. From fundamentals to advanced project design.

🔹 Foundation Level

  • Basic electrical concepts (voltage, current, power, resistance)
  • Understanding electrical symbols and diagrams
  • Introduction to design standards (IEC, NEC, IEEE)
  • CAD fundamentals (AutoCAD, EPLAN basics)
  • Safety awareness and design for safety (LOTO, segregation)

🔹 Intermediate Level

  • Creating single-line and schematic diagrams
  • Panel design and component selection (circuit breakers, contactors, PLCs)
  • Cable sizing and routing (trays, conduits, duct banks)
  • Earthing and lightning protection design
  • Lighting design calculations (Dialux/Relux)
  • Power distribution and load calculations

🔹 Advanced Level

  • Control system architecture (PLC, DCS, SCADA integration)
  • Protection coordination and selectivity studies
  • Design for hazardous areas (ATEX, IECEx)
  • Power system analysis (ETAP, SKM)
  • 3D panel design and virtual commissioning
  • Project management and documentation standards

🔹 Continuous Learning

  • Stay updated with IEC updates and new technologies
  • Attend design software training (AutoCAD, EPLAN, ETAP)
  • Certifications (IEC 60364, NFPA 70, IEEE 80)
  • Participate in design reviews and project collaboration
