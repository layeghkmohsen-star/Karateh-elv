📝 Design Examples

Practical projects and examples to apply engineering design knowledge. Includes complete descriptions and learning outcomes.

🔹 Example 1: Industrial Panel Design

Description: Design of a motor control panel for a 3-phase induction motor (45 kW, 400 V). Includes main circuit breaker, contactor, overload relay, PLC interface, and local controls.

  • Motor: 45 kW, 400 V, 3-phase
  • Panel: IP54 floor-standing enclosure
  • Key skills: Schematic drawing, panel layout, component selection, protection coordination

🔹 Example 2: Cable Routing for Power and Control

Description: Routing of power cables (3x185 mm²) and control cables (24x1.5 mm²) in a chemical plant using cable trays and conduits. Includes segregation, bend radius, and earthing requirements.

  • Power cable: 3x185 mm² XLPE
  • Control cable: 24x1.5 mm² multicore
  • Key skills: Cable sizing, tray design, segregation, installation standards

🔹 Example 3: Earthing System Design

Description: Design of an earthing system for a substation using a ring earth electrode, earthing conductors, and measurement points. Includes calculation of earth resistance and step/touch voltage verification.

  • Earth resistance target: < 1 Ω
  • Electrode: Copper-bonded rods
  • Key skills: Earth resistance calculation, IEEE 80, testing methods

🔹 Example 4: Lighting Design for Workshop

Description: Design of a lighting system for a 500 m² workshop with a target of 500 lux at workbenches. Includes luminaire selection, spacing, and uniformity checks.

  • Area: 500 m² workshop
  • Target illuminance: 500 lux
  • Key skills: Illuminance calculation, Dialux, energy efficiency
