📘 Overview of Engineering Design

A comprehensive overview of engineering design for industrial projects, covering schematics, panel design, cable routing, earthing, power distribution, and control systems.

🔹 What is Engineering Design?

Engineering design is the process of creating technical drawings, specifications, and plans for industrial electrical and control systems. It includes schematic diagrams, panel layouts, cable schedules, earthing designs, and power distribution schemes. The goal is to ensure safe, reliable, and efficient operation of electrical and automation systems.

🔹 Key Areas

  • Electrical schematic design and wiring diagrams
  • Panel design and component layout
  • Cable routing and tray systems
  • Earthing and lightning protection
  • Power distribution and load calculations
  • Control system architecture (PLC, DCS)

🔹 Key Standards

  • IEC 60364 – Electrical Installations
  • IEC 61439 – Low-voltage Switchgear
  • IEEE 80 – Earthing Design
  • NFPA 70 (NEC) – National Electrical Code
  • ISA 5.1 – Instrumentation Symbols
