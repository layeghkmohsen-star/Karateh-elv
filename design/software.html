KARATECH ELV

🛠️ Engineering Design Software

Essential software tools for electrical and control system design, including schematic drawing, panel design, lighting calculation, and simulation.

🔹 Electrical CAD Software

  • AutoCAD Electrical – Industry-standard for electrical schematics, panel layouts, and cable diagrams.
  • EPLAN Electric P8 – Advanced engineering design with integrated project management.
  • SEE Electrical – Powerful and intuitive electrical CAD for automation and control.
  • Elecdes Design Suite – Comprehensive electrical design tools.

🔹 Panel Design & Layout

  • EPLAN Pro Panel – 3D panel design and cabinet layout.
  • AutoCAD Electrical – Includes panel layout and component placement.
  • SolidWorks Electrical – 3D electrical design and routing.

🔹 Lighting & Power Calculation

  • Dialux – Lighting design and calculation software.
  • Relux – Indoor and outdoor lighting design.
  • ETAP – Power system analysis and calculation.
  • SKM PowerTools – Load flow, short circuit, and protection coordination.

🔹 Example: Full Design Workflow

A typical design project starts with AutoCAD Electrical for schematic drawings, EPLAN Pro Panel for 3D panel layout, and ETAP for power system verification. All files are managed in a common project repository for collaboration and version control.
