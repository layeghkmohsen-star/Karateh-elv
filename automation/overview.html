📘 Overview of Automation Engineering

A comprehensive overview of automation engineering, covering PLC, HMI, SCADA, industrial networks, and control systems.

🔹 What is Automation Engineering?

Automation engineering combines control systems, instrumentation, and software to create intelligent industrial systems that operate with minimal human intervention. It is the backbone of modern manufacturing and process industries.

🔹 Key Areas

  • PLC Programming (Ladder, SCL, FBD)
  • HMI/SCADA Design
  • Industrial Networks (Profinet, Modbus)
  • PID Control and Tuning
  • Motion Control and Robotics

🔹 Key Standards

  • IEC 61131-3 – PLC Programming Standards
  • IEC 61508 – Functional Safety
  • ISA 88 – Batch Control
