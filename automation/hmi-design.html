🖥️ HMI Design

Human-Machine Interface (HMI) design principles, software tools, and best practices for creating effective operator interfaces.

🔹 What is HMI Design?

HMI (Human-Machine Interface) design involves creating graphical interfaces that allow operators to monitor and control industrial processes. Good HMI design improves usability, reduces errors, and enhances operator efficiency.

🔹 Key Principles

  • Simplicity – Avoid clutter, show only necessary information
  • Consistency – Use uniform colors, fonts, and layouts
  • Feedback – Provide clear visual and audible feedback for actions
  • Alarm Management – Prioritize and group alarms logically
  • Navigation – Intuitive and easy to navigate screens
