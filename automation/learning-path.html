🗺️ Automation Learning Path

A structured learning path to become a skilled automation engineer. From foundations to advanced topics.

🔹 Foundation Level

  • Basic electricity and electronics
  • Introduction to PLC and control systems
  • Understanding sensors and actuators
  • Basic programming concepts
  • Safety fundamentals (LOTO, risk assessment)

🔹 Intermediate Level

  • PLC programming (Ladder, FBD, ST)
  • HMI design and SCADA systems
  • Industrial networks (Profinet, Modbus)
  • PID control and tuning
  • Motion control and robotics basics

🔹 Advanced Level

  • Multi-axis motion control and robotics
  • Advanced SCADA and IIoT
  • Functional safety (IEC 61508, ISO 13849)
  • MES and Industry 4.0
  • Project management and system integration

🔹 Continuous Learning

  • Stay updated with new technologies (AI, digital twin)
  • Attend conferences (Hannover Messe, SPS)
  • Certifications (ISA, Siemens, Rockwell)
  • Online courses (Udemy, Coursera, PLC Academy)
