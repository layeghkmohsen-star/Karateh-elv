🎬 Training Videos

A curated collection of training and tutorial videos for electrical engineering, automation, instrumentation, and control systems.

🔹 Why Video Training?

Video tutorials are one of the most effective ways to learn engineering concepts, software tools, and practical skills. They provide visual demonstrations, step-by-step instructions, and real-world examples that help bridge the gap between theory and practice. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced professional, video training can enhance your knowledge and keep you up-to-date with the latest technologies.

🔹 How to Use Video Resources

  • For Beginners – Start with introductory videos on basic concepts (PLC, HMI, SCADA)
  • For Intermediate Users – Watch advanced tutorials on specific topics like analog scaling, PID tuning, or network configuration
  • For Professionals – Explore webinars, case studies, and industry trends to stay updated
  • For Certification Prep – Use videos as part of your study plan for Siemens, Rockwell, or ISA certifications
  • For Troubleshooting – Watch practical troubleshooting videos to learn how to diagnose and fix common issues

🔹 Example: Learning PLC Programming with RealPars

A junior engineer wants to learn PLC programming. They subscribe to the RealPars YouTube channel and watch the "PLC Programming for Beginners" playlist. They learn about ladder logic, timers, counters, and analog scaling. After watching the videos, they practice on a PLC simulator to reinforce their understanding.
