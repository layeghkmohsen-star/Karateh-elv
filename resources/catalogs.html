📖 Industrial Catalogs

A curated collection of industrial product catalogs from leading manufacturers for electrical, automation, instrumentation, and control systems.

🔹 What are Industrial Catalogs?

Industrial catalogs are comprehensive documents published by manufacturers that list and describe their products, including specifications, technical data, application examples, and ordering information. They are essential resources for engineers, designers, and procurement professionals to select the right components for their projects.

🔹 Major Manufacturers

  • Siemens – Automation, drives, switchgear, PLCs, SCADA
  • Allen-Bradley (Rockwell) – PLCs, HMIs, drives, safety systems
  • Schneider Electric – Switchgear, PLCs, HMIs, sensors
  • Mitsubishi Electric – PLCs, HMIs, servos, drives
  • ABB – Motors, drives, switchgear, robots
  • Emerson – Instrumentation, control valves, analyzers
  • Endress+Hauser – Instrumentation, sensors, transmitters
  • Rosemount – Pressure, temperature, level, flow transmitters
  • Weintek – HMIs, touch panels
  • Omron – PLCs, sensors, relays, safety components

🔹 How to Use Catalogs

  • Product Selection – Compare technical specifications to choose the best product
  • Application Guidance – Understand where and how to use specific products
  • Technical Data – Access dimensions, ratings, wiring diagrams, and performance curves
  • Ordering Information – Find part numbers and ordering codes
  • Compatibility – Check compatibility with other components and systems

🔹 Example: Finding the Right Motor Starter

An engineer uses a Siemens catalog to select a motor starter for a 45 kW induction motor. The catalog provides the technical data for different starter types, including direct-on-line, star-delta, and soft starters. After comparing features, the engineer selects a soft starter based on the motor's full-load current and starting torque requirements.
