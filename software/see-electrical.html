🔌 SEE Electrical

SEE Electrical is a powerful and intuitive electrical CAD software designed for automation, control, and power distribution projects.

🔹 Description

SEE Electrical is a professional electrical CAD solution that helps engineers and designers create electrical schematics, wiring diagrams, and panel layouts quickly and efficiently. It is known for its user-friendly interface, automation features, and integration with other engineering tools.

🔹 Purpose

The purpose of SEE Electrical is to streamline the design of electrical control systems, from schematic capture to manufacturing documentation. It reduces design time, improves accuracy, and ensures compliance with industry standards (IEC, NEC, etc.). It is widely used in automation, machinery, and building services.

🔹 Example

A control system designer uses SEE Electrical to develop the electrical design for an HVAC control panel. They create schematics for power distribution, motor control, and PLC I/O. The software automatically numbers wires, generates cross-references, and produces a complete bill of materials.

🔹 Learning Path

  • Introduction to SEE Electrical interface and project setup
  • Creating electrical schematics and using symbol libraries
  • Wiring diagrams and cable management
  • Panel design and component placement
  • Generating reports (BOM, wire lists, terminal diagrams)
