📚 HSE Resources

Books, websites, standards, and educational materials for health, safety, and environmental management.

🔹 Recommended Books

  • Safety Management – John R. Grimaldi
  • Environmental Management: Principles and Practice – C. R. Boyce
  • Occupational Health and Safety – C. G. Brown
  • Risk Assessment and Management – David H. Johnson
  • Industrial Safety and Health Management – C. Ray Asfahl

🔹 Useful Websites and Organizations

  • OSHA – Occupational Safety and Health Administration – osha.gov
  • WHO – World Health Organization – who.int
  • ISO – International Organization for Standardization – iso.org
  • NEBOSH – National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health – nebosh.org.uk
  • AIHA – American Industrial Hygiene Association – aiha.org
  • EPA – Environmental Protection Agency – epa.gov

🔹 Key HSE Standards

  • ISO 45001 – Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems
  • ISO 14001 – Environmental Management Systems
  • ISO 31000 – Risk Management
  • IEC 61508 – Functional Safety
  • NFPA 70E – Electrical Safety in the Workplace
  • OHSAS 18001 – Occupational Health and Safety (replaced by ISO 45001)
