🔹 Example: Using Gensuite for Safety Management

A multinational company uses Gensuite for incident reporting, safety observations, and corrective action tracking. Employees report near-misses via mobile app, and safety managers analyze trends to identify high-risk areas. The platform integrates with training records and compliance calendars to ensure continuous improvement.
