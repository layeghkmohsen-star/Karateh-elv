💻 Standards Software

Essential software tools for managing, searching, and applying engineering standards across multiple disciplines.

🔹 Standards Management Software

  • IHS Markit Standards Store – Comprehensive access to international standards (IEC, ISO, API, etc.)
  • Accuris (Techstreet) – Standards management, subscription, and compliance tracking
  • BSOL – British Standards Online – Access to British and international standards
  • NEMA Standards Store – Standards for electrical equipment
  • ANSI Webstore – Purchasing and accessing ANSI standards

🔹 Standards Search & Reference Tools

  • IEC Webstore – Search and purchase IEC standards
  • ISO Store – Access ISO standards online
  • IEEE Xplore – Access IEEE standards and publications
  • API Standards Store – API standards for oil and gas
  • NFPA Catalog – NEC and fire safety standards

🔹 Compliance & Audit Software

  • ETQ Reliance – Compliance management and standards tracking
  • Gensuite – Regulatory compliance and audit management
  • MasterControl – Document control and compliance with ISO 9001/ISO 13485
  • AssurX – Quality and compliance management

🔹 Example: Using IHS Markit for Standards Compliance

An electrical engineer uses IHS Markit to access the latest IEC standards for a new project. The platform provides real-time updates, allowing the team to ensure compliance with the latest revisions of IEC 60364 and IEC 61439. The project team also uses the platform to track future revisions and maintain documentation.
