💻 Quality Control Software

Essential software tools for quality control, including SPC, QMS, audit management, and data analysis platforms.

🔹 Statistical Process Control (SPC) Software

  • Minitab – Comprehensive statistical analysis and SPC
  • SPSS – Advanced analytics and data mining
  • Q-DAS – Automotive-grade SPC and quality data management
  • NCSS – Statistical analysis and data visualization

🔹 Quality Management System (QMS) Software

  • MasterControl – QMS, document control, training management
  • ETQ Reliance – Enterprise quality and compliance management
  • Qualio – Cloud-based QMS for startups and SMEs
  • Greenlight Guru – QMS for medical devices

🔹 Audit and Inspection Management

  • Gensuite – Audit management, corrective action tracking
  • Intelex – EHS and quality audit software
  • Cority – Audit, compliance, and risk management
  • SafetyCulture iAuditor – Mobile inspection checklists

🔹 Example: Quality Data Analysis with Minitab

A quality engineer uses Minitab to analyze data from a production line. They create control charts, perform process capability analysis (Cp, Cpk), and conduct root cause analysis using cause-and-effect diagrams. The software provides actionable insights and reports for management review.
