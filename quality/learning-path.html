🗺️ Quality Control Learning Path

A structured learning path to become a skilled quality professional. From fundamentals to advanced quality management.

🔹 Foundation Level

  • Basic understanding of quality concepts and terminology
  • Introduction to quality management systems (QMS)
  • Understanding inspection and testing methods
  • Basic statistics for quality (mean, median, standard deviation)
  • Introduction to ISO 9001 and quality standards

🔹 Intermediate Level

  • Implementing SPC and control charts (X-bar, R, p, np charts)
  • Process capability analysis (Cp, Cpk, Pp, Ppk)
  • Conducting internal audits and corrective actions
  • Using the 7 QC tools for problem-solving
  • Introduction to Six Sigma and DMAIC methodology

🔹 Advanced Level

  • Advanced statistical methods (DOE, hypothesis testing)
  • Six Sigma Black Belt level tools and techniques
  • Quality risk management (FMEA, HAZOP)
  • Supply chain quality management and supplier audits
  • Quality leadership and continuous improvement culture

🔹 Continuous Learning

  • Stay updated with ISO revisions and quality trends
  • Attend quality conferences and workshops (ASQ, ISO)
  • Certifications (ASQ CQE, Six Sigma Green/Black Belt)
  • Join professional networks and communities of practice
