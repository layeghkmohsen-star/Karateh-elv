🔹 What's Next in Quality Control?

The future of quality control will be driven by automation, AI, and data analytics. Quality professionals will use predictive analytics to prevent defects, cloud-based systems for real-time quality management, and blockchain for traceability. Continuous improvement will become more data-driven and integrated across the value chain.
