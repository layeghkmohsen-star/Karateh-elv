📝 Quality Control Examples

Practical projects and examples to apply quality control knowledge. Includes complete descriptions and learning outcomes.

🔹 Example 1: SPC Implementation

Description: Implementation of Statistical Process Control (SPC) on a production line to monitor product weight. Includes control chart creation, capability analysis, and corrective actions.

  • Process: Filling machine for 1kg bags
  • Tools: X-bar and R charts, Minitab
  • Key skills: Data collection, control limits, process capability (Cp, Cpk)

🔹 Example 2: ISO 9001 Internal Audit

Description: Conduct an internal audit of the Quality Management System (QMS) to prepare for ISO 9001 recertification. Includes audit planning, execution, reporting, and corrective actions.

  • Scope: Full QMS covering all departments
  • Audit team: Internal auditors, lead auditor
  • Key skills: Audit planning, interviewing, non-conformity reporting

🔹 Example 3: Six Sigma Project

Description: A Six Sigma project using the DMAIC methodology to reduce defects in a PCB assembly process. Includes definition, measurement, analysis, improvement, and control phases.

  • Problem: 5% defect rate in PCB soldering
  • Tools: DMAIC, Pareto, Fishbone, control charts
  • Key skills: Root cause analysis, improvement implementation, sustainability

🔹 Example 4: Quality Audit of Supplier

Description: Perform a quality audit of a critical supplier for a manufacturing company. Includes reviewing their QMS, inspection records, and corrective action history.

  • Supplier: Component manufacturer
  • Audit criteria: ISO 9001 compliance, quality records
  • Key skills: Supplier audit, risk assessment, improvement planning
