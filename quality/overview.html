📘 Overview of Quality Control

A comprehensive overview of quality control principles, systems, tools, and standards for engineering and manufacturing.

🔹 What is Quality Control?

Quality control (QC) is a process by which entities review the quality of all factors involved in production. It involves inspection, testing, and corrective actions to ensure that products and services meet specified requirements. QC is essential for customer satisfaction, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement.

🔹 Key Areas of Quality Control

  • Quality Management Systems (QMS) – ISO 9001
  • Inspection and Testing – Incoming, in-process, final
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC) – Monitoring process stability
  • Quality Audits – Internal and external assessments
  • Continuous Improvement – Six Sigma, Lean
  • Quality Tools – 7 QC tools (Pareto, Fishbone, etc.)

🔹 Key Quality Standards

  • ISO 9001 – Quality Management Systems
  • ISO 14001 – Environmental Management
  • ISO 45001 – Occupational Health and Safety
  • IEC 61508 – Functional Safety
  • API Q1 – Quality Management for Oil and Gas
