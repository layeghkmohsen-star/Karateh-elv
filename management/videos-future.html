🔹 What's Next in Engineering Management?

The future of engineering management will be shaped by digital transformation, data-driven decision-making, and flexible project management approaches. Managers will leverage AI for predictive analytics, virtual collaboration tools for remote teams, and agile frameworks for faster project delivery. The integration of sustainability and ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) factors will also become critical.
