💧 Flow Measurement

Methods and devices for measuring flow rate in industrial processes: orifice plates, magnetic, ultrasonic, Coriolis, and more.

🔹 Importance of Flow Measurement

Flow measurement is critical in industries such as water treatment, oil & gas, chemical, and food processing. Accurate flow measurement ensures proper dosing, process efficiency, and regulatory compliance.

🔹 Common Flow Meters

  • Orifice Plate – Differential pressure based, simple and low cost
  • Magnetic Flowmeter – For conductive liquids, no moving parts
  • Ultrasonic Flowmeter – Clamp-on or inline, for clean liquids
  • Coriolis Flowmeter – Mass flow measurement, high accuracy
  • Vortex Flowmeter – For steam, gas, and liquids

🔹 Example: Magnetic Flowmeter for Water Line

A 4-inch magnetic flowmeter is installed on a water supply line in a manufacturing plant. It measures flow rate in the range of 0-100 m³/h and outputs 4-20 mA to the PLC. The flowmeter is used for batching and totalization.
