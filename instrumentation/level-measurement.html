📏 Level Measurement

Methods and devices for measuring liquid and solid levels in tanks, silos, and vessels: radar, ultrasonic, capacitance, and more.

🔹 Importance of Level Measurement

Level measurement is essential in industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, food & beverage, and oil & gas. Accurate level measurement ensures inventory control, process efficiency, and safety (preventing overfills and dry runs).

🔹 Common Level Instruments

  • Radar Level Transmitter – Non-contact, guided wave, for liquids and solids
  • Ultrasonic Level Transmitter – Non-contact, for liquids and slurries
  • Capacitance Level Transmitter – For conductive and non-conductive liquids
  • Float Switch – Simple on/off level detection
  • Hydrostatic Pressure Transmitter – Measures level by pressure at bottom of tank

🔹 Example: Radar Level Transmitter for Water Tank

A radar level transmitter is installed at the top of a 20-meter water storage tank. It uses guided wave radar technology to measure the distance to the water surface and outputs 4-20 mA to the SCADA system. The transmitter is used for inventory monitoring and pump control.
