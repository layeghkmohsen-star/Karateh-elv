📟 Sensors & Transmitters

An overview of industrial sensors and transmitters used to measure temperature, pressure, flow, level, and other process variables.

🔹 What are Sensors and Transmitters?

Sensors detect physical or chemical changes in the process (temperature, pressure, flow, level, etc.) and convert them into electrical signals. Transmitters amplify, convert, and transmit these signals to control systems (PLC, DCS, SCADA).

🔹 Common Sensor Types

  • Temperature Sensors – RTD, Thermocouple, Thermistor
  • Pressure Sensors – Piezoresistive, Capacitive, Strain Gauge
  • Flow Sensors – Orifice, Magnetic, Ultrasonic, Coriolis
  • Level Sensors – Radar, Ultrasonic, Capacitance, Float
  • Analytical Sensors – pH, Conductivity, Dissolved Oxygen
