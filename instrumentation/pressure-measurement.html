📊 Pressure Measurement

Methods and devices for measuring pressure in industrial processes: transmitters, gauges, switches, and differential pressure.

🔹 Importance of Pressure Measurement

Pressure is a fundamental process variable in industries such as oil & gas, chemical, power generation, and HVAC. Accurate pressure measurement ensures safe operation, process efficiency, and equipment protection.

🔹 Common Pressure Instruments

  • Pressure Transmitter – Converts pressure to 4-20 mA signal (e.g., Rosemount 3051)
  • Pressure Gauge – Mechanical or digital display for local reading
  • Pressure Switch – Provides on/off contact at setpoint
  • Differential Pressure (DP) Transmitter – Measures pressure difference for flow or level

🔹 Example: Pressure Transmitter for Tank

A pressure transmitter installed at the bottom of a storage tank measures hydrostatic pressure to determine liquid level. The transmitter outputs 4-20 mA to the DCS, which calculates the level based on density and pressure.
