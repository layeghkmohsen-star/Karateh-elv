Commissioning is the final phase of a project where the installed systems are tested, verified, and handed over to the client for operation after demonstrating that they meet all requirements.

рҹ”№ What is Commissioning?

Commissioning is the systematic process of ensuring that all systems and components of a plant or facility are installed, tested, and operated according to the design intent and client requirements. It bridges the gap between construction and operation, verifying that everything works as intended and that operators are trained to run the facility safely and efficiently.

рҹ”№ Key Commissioning Activities

  • Pre-commissioning вҖ“ Pre-checks, flushing, pressure testing, and cleaning before system startup
  • Start-up вҖ“ Energizing systems, checking rotation, and bringing online
  • Functional Testing вҖ“ Testing each system under normal and abnormal conditions
  • Performance Testing вҖ“ Verifying capacity, efficiency, and reliability against specifications
  • Safety Testing вҖ“ Testing safety systems, interlocks, and emergency shutdown systems
  • Training вҖ“ Training operators and maintenance personnel on system operation and troubleshooting
  • Handover вҖ“ Formal transfer of the system to the client with all documentation, certificates, and approvals

рҹ”№ Commissioning Documentation

  • Commissioning Plan вҖ“ Detailed schedule, procedures, and responsibilities
  • Test Procedures вҖ“ Step-by-step instructions for each test
  • Test Reports вҖ“ Records of all test results and deviations
  • As-built Drawings вҖ“ Updated drawings reflecting actual installation
  • Operation and Maintenance Manuals вҖ“ Comprehensive guides for operators
  • Certificate of Acceptance вҖ“ Formal handover document signed by client

рҹ”№ Example: Commissioning a Gas Turbine Power Plant

A gas turbine power plant is commissioned in three phases: (1) Pre-commissioning вҖ“ flushing oil systems, pressure testing, and inspecting all components, (2) Start-up вҖ“ first firing, synchronization with the grid, and load testing, (3) Performance testing вҖ“ verifying output, heat rate, and emissions at full load. The commissioning team documents all results, trains the operations team, and handovers the plant to the client.
